Comprehensive Analysis

The Green Forward pre-conference workshop demonstrates exceptional engagement and strategic alignment with green economy objectives. With 15 attendees maintaining near-perfect retention (93.3% Day 2 attendance), the program attracted experienced professionals averaging 36.5 years of age with balanced gender representation. The participant profile reveals a strong foundation for impact, with NGOs comprising 53% of organizations and 73% already supporting green SMEs. Notably, 60% of participating organizations bring 5+ years of SME support experience, while capacity building emerges as the primary objective for over half the participants. The expertise levels are encouraging, with most organizations self-rating at Level 4-5 for green economy understanding, indicating both competence and awareness of growth opportunities. This combination of experience, commitment, and clear developmental goals positions the workshop attendees as ideal catalysts for advancing green economy initiatives within their respective organizations and communities.
